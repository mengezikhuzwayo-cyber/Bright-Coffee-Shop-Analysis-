# Bright-Coffee-Shop-Analysis-
 Data analysis for sales of Bright Coffee Shop was conducted to check the performance of different stores, checking transaction history to extract accurate insight and present to the CEO for better decision making of the company.

## Introduction
The dataset used in this project contains daily transactional records from a coffee shop. A new CEO has been appointed with the goal of increasing revenue and improving product performance. As a Junior Data Analyst, this project aims to extract insights from historical data and present findings that will guide business decisions.

## Problem Statement 
The coffee shop lacks clear visibility into its sales performance, including which products generate the most revenue, what times of day drive the most sales, and how product demand changes over time. Without these insights, it is difficult for management to make informed decisions to grow the business.

## Aim 
To analyze historical sales (6 motnhs from Janaury to June)  data and provide actionable insights that will help improve revenue, optimize product performance, and support data-driven decision-making


## Objectives 
+ Identify top-performing products based on revenue
+ Determine peak sales periods during the day
+ Analyze sales trends across different product categories
+ Calculate total revenue using transactional data
+ Provide recommendations to improve overall sales performance


## Summary Of Results 
+ High-performing products were identified as key revenue drivers
+ Peak sales were observed during specific time intervals
+ Certain products showed consistently low performance, indicating opportunities for promotion or replacement
+ Sales trends revealed patterns that can guide inventory and marketing strategies
+ Recommendations were provided to optimize product offerings, improve marketing during slow periods, and increase overall efficiency
